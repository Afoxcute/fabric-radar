About Fabric Radar

Our mission is to revolutionize the tailoring industry through blockchain technology

Fabric Radar was born from a simple observation: skilled tailors with smartphones weren't connecting with potential clients effectively. Traditional social media platforms were too distracting, and the cost of transportation for in-person meetings was prohibitive.

Our solution leverages the Stellar blockchain to create a trusted platform that connects tailors with clients, providing a secure and seamless experience. By tokenizing fabrics as NFTs and using smart contracts for agreements, we've eliminated common pain points in the industry.

We're committed to supporting local tailors while giving clients access to quality custom clothing, all within a user-friendly interface that makes blockchain technology accessible to everyone.
